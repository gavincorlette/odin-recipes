Lasagna

Description

This is going to be an amazing lasagna dish for you and your whole family to enjoy!

It's simple to make and delicious!

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Pasta
  • Tomato Puree
  • Oregano
  • Parmesean Cheese

Steps

  1. Prep pasta layers into sections
  2. Spread tomato paste on each layer of pasta
  3. Put meat and cheese in layers as desired
  4. Cook in oven for 25 minutes
  5. Serve and enjoy!
