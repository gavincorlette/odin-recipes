Tacos

Description

This is a recipe for simple beef tacos!

Ingredients

  • Taco Shells
  • Ground Beef
  • Lettuce
  • Salsa
  • Shredded Cheese
  • Jalapenos

Steps

  1. Cook beef in a pan and season
  2. Cook taco shells in overn
  3. Layer cheese at bottom of taco shell
  4. Then add ground beef
  5. Add salsa
  6. Add jalpenos
  7. Add lettuce
  8. Serve!
